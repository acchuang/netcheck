About NetCheck

Local diagnostics for your network connection and browser security.

DNS Check

Detects your IP address, DNS resolver configuration, and checks DNS security features such as DNSSEC validation, DoH encryption, and malware filtering. Includes a DNS lookup tool to query domains for any record type.

Speed Test

Measures download and upload speed, latency, jitter, and bufferbloat using your nearest Cloudflare edge server. Displays real-time performance graphs, a 5-factor quality grade, and keeps a persistent history of your last results.

Ad Block Test

Checks how well your ad blocker or browser is blocking ad scripts, trackers, analytics, social media widgets, and fingerprinting scripts. Gives a score from 0 to 100 with category-specific recommendations.

Headers

Scans any website for 10 critical security headers: HSTS, CSP, X-Frame-Options, Referrer-Policy, and more. Provides a letter grade, detailed per-header analysis, and suggests improvements.

Fingerprint

Analyzes how unique your browser fingerprint is across 6 categories: Canvas, WebGL, Audio, Fonts, Screen, and Navigator properties. Provides tailored privacy protection tips to reduce your online trackability.

Connection Quality

Inspects your browser connection info (type, downlink, RTT), TLS details, request timing breakdown (DNS / TCP / TLS / TTFB / Download), and runs a stability test measuring jitter and packet loss over 30 pings. Computes an overall A+–F grade.

Network Map

Measures your latency to global network endpoints, giving a regional view of connection health. Visualizes closest regions, quality ratings, and server relay latency via Cloudflare’s distributed edge.
